# Accessibility Statement

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2026

ARRVL Studio is committed to making our website accessible and usable for as many people as possible.

We aim to follow recognized accessibility standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, where reasonably achievable. Our goal is to provide a website experience that is clear, navigable, and usable across different devices, browsers, and assistive technologies.

## Our Efforts

We work to improve accessibility by considering:

- Clear page structure and headings
- Keyboard navigability
- Sufficient color contrast
- Readable typography
- Descriptive links and buttons
- Alternative text for meaningful images
- Responsive layouts across devices
- Compatibility with modern assistive technologies

## Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As our website changes, we may update content, design, code, or functionality to improve usability and address accessibility issues.

## Third-Party Content

Some parts of our website may use third-party tools, embeds, platforms, or integrations that we do not fully control. While we aim to work with providers that support accessibility, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content or services.

## Feedback

If you experience difficulty using our website or notice an accessibility issue, contact us so we can review it.
